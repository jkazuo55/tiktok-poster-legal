Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how VideoAutoPost ("the App") handles information. The App is a personal automation tool designed for a single user to publish videos to their own TikTok account via the official TikTok API.

2. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or process personal data from third parties. The only data handled by the App is:

  • TikTok API credentials: OAuth tokens required to authenticate the user's own TikTok account.
  • Video files: Content provided by the user for upload to TikTok.

3. How We Use Information

All information is used solely for the purpose of uploading and publishing videos to the user's own TikTok account. No data is shared with, sold to, or disclosed to any third party.

4. Data Storage

Authentication tokens are stored locally on the user's own device. The App does not use external databases, analytics services, or cloud storage for user data.

5. Third-Party Services

The App communicates exclusively with the official TikTok API to perform its functions. The use of TikTok's API is governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy and Developer Terms. No other third-party services are used.

6. Data Retention

The App does not retain video files or personal data after a video has been successfully uploaded. OAuth tokens remain stored locally until the user revokes access or deletes them.

7. User Rights

Since all data is stored locally and the App operates on a single-user basis, the user has full control over their data at all times. To revoke access, simply remove the App's authorization from your TikTok account settings.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. No data from children is knowingly collected.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
